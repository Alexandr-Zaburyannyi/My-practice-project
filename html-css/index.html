We wanted to use some of the best input materials on the market for this watch.
Inside of the STRIDER
watches are the Swiss
Made Sellita SW 200-1.
They are hugely employed by the most renowned firms in the field of horology. They are very accurate and built to last for many years. This caliber is considered a workhorse by today's watch making standard.

A classy and
highly luminous
dial.

The case is made of 316 L
stainless steel which is also
called as surgical steel. This is a
high grade, metallurgically strong
steel that has a greater resistant
to corrosives. Due to its
effectiveness in high-stress
situations, more specifically in
contexts that present remarkable
levels of acidity, it is highly
appreciated in marine
environments.

Sapphire
Crystal Glass.
Five layers of anti-reflective
coating are added to the
underside of the crystal in
addition to the double dome,
ensuring a high degree of clarity
and minimal light distortion.
The crystal is 3.5 mm thick in the center and tapers
down to 3.3 mm at the sides. This enables water tight
setup for the watch at all circumstances.
